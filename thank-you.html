If you applied as a surrogate

📧
Within 48 hours

Partner agencies receive and review your application. You may get initial outreach by email.

📞
Within 1 week

Qualified applicants are contacted for a phone screening to discuss your goals and surrogacy timeline.

🏥
Within 2–4 weeks

If accepted, you'll complete medical and psychological evaluations — all costs are covered by the intended parents.

⚖️
Legal agreements (2–3 months)

An attorney (paid by the intended parents) will review and finalize your surrogacy contract.

💰
Timeline to first compensation

Surrogates typically begin receiving compensation within 18–24 months of starting the process. Monthly payments begin after embryo transfer.

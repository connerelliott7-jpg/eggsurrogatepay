⚠️ Surrogacy Laws in Nebraska

Commercial surrogacy has significant legal restrictions in Nebraska. Surrogacy contracts may not be enforceable. Residents interested in surrogacy typically work with agencies and attorneys in more surrogacy-friendly states.

We recommend consulting with a reproductive attorney if you are interested in surrogacy as a Nebraska resident.

Egg Donation Is Available in Nebraska

Egg donation is fully legal in Nebraska. First-time egg donors earn $7,000–$12,000, plus travel and expense reimbursement.

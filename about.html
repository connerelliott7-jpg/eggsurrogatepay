About EggSurrogatePay.com

We built the compensation transparency resource we wish existed when we started researching this process.

Our Mission

Egg donation and surrogacy are deeply meaningful — and they deserve transparency. For too long, compensation information has been scattered, outdated, or deliberately vague. Agencies benefit from information asymmetry. Prospective donors and surrogates deserve better.

EggSurrogatePay.com exists to give prospective egg donors and surrogates a clear, honest picture of what they can realistically earn — before they fill out a single agency form. Our free calculators use real market data from 30+ agencies to give you a personalized estimate in under 60 seconds.

✅ What We Are

  • →An independent research and referral platform
  • →A free compensation calculator built on real agency data
  • →A bridge connecting qualified candidates with licensed agencies
  • →Committed to transparency about how we make money
  • →Advocates for fair, informed participation in the industry

❌ What We Are Not

  • →A surrogacy or egg donation agency
  • →A fertility clinic or medical provider
  • →A law firm or legal advisor
  • →An employer of donors or surrogates
  • →A party to any compensation agreement

How We Work

When you submit an application through our site, we share your information with 3–5 licensed Partner Agencies in your area. Those agencies review your application and reach out to qualified candidates for further screening. We earn a referral fee when a qualified lead is delivered — this is how we keep our calculators and resources free for everyone.

Our commercial relationships with agencies do not influence the compensation data we publish. Estimates are based on independent market research, not what agencies ask us to display. See our Methodology for full details.

Our Principles

🔍

Transparency First

We publish our methodology, data sources, and commercial relationships openly. You should know exactly how our estimates are built and how we make money.

⚖️

Accuracy Over Optimism

We'd rather show realistic ranges than inflate numbers to drive more applications. Our estimates are calibrated against real agency data, not marketing copy.

🛡️

Candidate First

We only work with licensed, reputable agencies. Your data is never sold to data brokers. You can request deletion of your information at any time.
